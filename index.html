Only the features you need.

Simplenote is designed to get out of the way so you can be more productive. We’ve emphasized speed and clarity, creating a beautiful writing app with zero bloat. Let your thoughts flow, free of distractions.

Sync notes across all your devices.

From the start, Simplenote was built with sync in mind. Type a note on your iPhone, watch it magically update on your Mac. Simplenote elegantly handles the compexity of managing updates and saving versions, so you can write with confidence.

Stay organized.

With instantaneous search, tags and full version history on each note, finding notes and keeping all of your work in order is a breeze.
